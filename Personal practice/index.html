In this work I wanted to bring in a visual performance through my personal experience of hiking. What I see when I hike, I take photographs about. What I hear, I recorded. Taking these abstract materials and composing them into a consistent visual performance.

Personal work goals

Introduction of work

I sought to translate the sights and sounds encountered along the trails into a tangible expression. Each photograph became a snapshot of a moment in time, preserving the beauty and serenity found in the wilderness. But the visual narrative didn't end there. Equipped with a keen ear for the nuances of nature's symphony, I recorded the melodic chirping of birds, the rustle of leaves in the wind, and the gentle babbling of streams. These auditory gems served as a soundtrack to accompany the visual feast, adding depth and dimension to the sensory experience.

Yet, it was the harmonious fusion of these abstract materials that truly brought my vision to life. Through meticulous editing and arrangement, I wove together the images and sounds, layering them in a seamless tapestry of sight and sound. The result was a mesmerizing visual performance that transported the audience to the heart of the wilderness, inviting them to embark on a sensory journey unlike any other.

In essence, my work served as a homage to the transformative power of nature, capturing not just its physical beauty, but also the profound sense of awe and wonder it inspires within us. Through the medium of visual performance, I hoped to evoke that same sense of connection and reverence for the natural world, inviting others to join me on a journey of exploration and discovery.
