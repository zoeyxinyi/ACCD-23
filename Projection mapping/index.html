          Projection mapping workshop

Digital Floor Screen

Showcase

I chose four videos of traditional Chinese stories to play on this one screen at the same time. To achieve a kind of real-time playing effect.

The lack of visual clutter or distractions allows the projection to take center stage, commanding the viewer's full attention. The boundaries between the digital work and the physical environment begin to dissolve, creating a seamless integration that blurs the lines between reality and the virtual realm. This fusion of art and environment transforms the projection into a living, breathing entity that the audience can fully engage with, immersing themselves in the sensory experience. No longer a passive observer, the viewer becomes an active participant, their movements and interactions shaping the evolution of the artwork in real-time.
